Welcome to Discover Scholarships

At DiscoverScholarships, we aim to provide affordable and equal education to anyone who seeks it. With college tuition increasing every year, prospective students are unable to attend these colleges without taking loans that put mountains of debt upon them. However, with DiscoverScholarships, you can no longer worry about money and instead use your own merit to help pay for college.

About Us

DiscoverScholarships is a nonprofit that aims to help those who need help paying for college. By providing the necessary tools to help students secure scholarships, DiscoverScholarships believes that education should be equitable for all students who seek it.

How DiscoverScholarships is different:

  • Provides unique, unheard-of scholarships in the DMV area that less students apply to
  • Uses an intelligent matching algorithm to choose the best scholarships
  • Completely free, DiscoverScholarships is here to assist you through the entire process

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does Discover Scholarships match scholarships to me?

    We use an artificial intelligence algorithm that predicts scholarship matches based on your personal and academic information and previous students' information.

  • Is DiscoverScholarships free?

    DiscoverScholarships is 100% free and will never charge a fee for any service

  • Why should I apply for scholarships?

    With increasing college tuition costs, higher education seems less and less attainable by the year. With scholarships, tuition costs can be lowered to an affordable amount

  • How will DiscoverScholarships help me with my application?

    DiscoverScholarships’ team will provide guidance in the essay process to help applicants stand out and give them the best chance to secure the scholarship

  • How can I contact DiscoverScholarships?

    Please visit the Contact Us page. You will always be contacting and speaking to a real person and never robots.
